Our Services

We offer quality services all year round!

Income Tax Return Preparation

Our office prepares tax returns for individuals with all forms of income: W-2, 1099, Schedule(s), Property Renters, ITIN application and renovation, and more. We also specialize in preparing returns for self employed individuals and small businesses. Our preparers dedicate the time to carefully understand each person’s unique situation and challenges, so that we can ensure that you receive all the deductions and credits you are entitled to. With us, the preparation process is careful and communicative for every client.

Notary Public & Apostille Services

Our certified public notaries verify the authenticity of various forms of documents and letters: Business Contracts, Permit to Travel, Power of Attorney, Proof of Residency, Acknowledgements, Court Documents, and more. We also provide assistance with obtaining or creating these documents as needed.

Bookkeeping Services

Our office manages and records the financial details of our clients’ business. We keep track of spending, payroll, and budget reports, create cost analyses, provide tax services, and manage other business details. We extract important insights from this financial data and work with our clients to optimize their financial transactions and keep their businesses thriving.
